Medicine - C

Cabaser - (Cabergoline)

Cabaser (Cabergoline) is used to treat the symptoms of Parkinson’s Disease. It is used after your Doctor has tried other treatments that have not worked or for people who are already taking other medicines for this illness to help control other symptoms.

Calcichew D3 Forte tablets

Calcichew D3 Forte (Calcium Carbonate) may be prescribed by doctors or recommended by pharmacists to treat and prevent calcium deficiency, or when the diet or lifestyle does not provide enough, or when body requirements are increased.

Calcichew tablets

Calcichew (Calcium Carbonate) may be prescribed by doctors or recommended by pharmacists to treat and prevent calcium deficiency, or when the diet or lifestyle does not provide enough, or when body requirements are increased.

Calcium

Calcium, the most abundant mineral in the body, is found in some foods, added to others, available as a dietary supplement, and present in some medicines (such as antacids).

Calpol Sugar Free (OTC)

Calpol contains the active ingredient paracetamol, a painkilling medicine used to relieve mild to moderate pain and fever - read on for advice on its use, warnings and side effectsabc

Calpol Suspension (OTC)

Calpol contains the active ingredient paracetamol, a painkilling medicine used to relieve mild to moderate pain and fever - read on for advice on its use, warnings and side effects.

Calpol Suspension (OTC)

Calpol contains the active ingredient paracetamol, a painkilling medicine used to relieve mild to moderate pain and fever - read on for advice on its use, warnings and side effects.

Calvapen - (Phenoxymethylpenicillin), penicillin v

Phenoxymethylpenicillin, commonly known as penicillin V, is a penicillin antibiotic that is orally active. It exerts a bactericidal action against penicillin-sensitive microorganisms during the stage of active multiplication. It acts by inhibiting the biosynthesis of cell-wall peptidoglycan.

Calvepen 250 Suspension

Phenoxymethylpenicillin, commonly known as penicillin V, is a penicillin antibiotic that is orally active. It exerts a bactericidal action against penicillin-sensitive microorganisms during the stage of active multiplication. It acts by inhibiting the biosynthesis of cell-wall peptidoglycan.

Camcolit Tablets

Camcolit tablets contain the active ingredient lithium carbonate, which is a type of medicine known as a mood stabiliser.

Candesartan Tablets

Candesartan is used for treating high blood pressure (hypertension) and heart failure.

Canesten 500mg Pessary

Canesten 500mg Pessary is recommended for the treatment of candidal vaginitis.

Canesten Cream

Canesten cream is to treat fungal infections like athlete's foot and thrush.

Canesten HC cream

Canesten HC cream contains two active ingredients, clotrimazole and hydrocortisone. Clotrimazole is an antifungal medicine used to treat infections with fungi and yeasts. Hydrocortisone is a corticosteroid medicine that is applied to the skin to relieve the symptoms of inflammation.

Capoten

Capoten (Captopril) is used to treat high blood pressure and certain heart conditions. Capoten can also help people whose heart no longer pumps blood as well as it once did. This condition is known as heart failure. Capoten may also be used to treat patients who recently suffered a heart attack.

Capozide

Capozide tablets are used in the treatment of hypertension (high blood pressure).

Caprin 300mg

Caprin (Caprin) has analgesic (pain killing), antipyretic (reduces temperature) and anti-inflammatory actions. It also has a blood thinning action, which has been shown to be useful following myocardial infarction, and in patients with unstable angina or ischaemic stroke.

Caprin 75mg Tablet

Caprin (Caprin) has a blood thinning action which has been shown to be useful following heart attack and in patients with unstable angina or ischaemic stroke. Caprin 75 is used wherever prolonged dosage of Caprin is required. Caprin 75 is unsuitable for the short-term relief of pain.

Cardicor (Bisoprolol)

Cardicor (Bisoprolol) is used in the treatment of stable heart failure i.e when the heart is not pumping enough blood to meet the body’s needs.

Cardura XL Tablets

Cardura is one of a group of medicines called alpha-blockers. It is used to treat high blood pressure, or the symptoms caused by enlargement of the prostate gland in men.
