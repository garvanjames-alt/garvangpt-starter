Efexor (venlafaxine) — quick notes

• Class: SNRI (serotonin–norepinephrine reuptake inhibitor).
• Uses: major depressive disorder, anxiety disorders, panic disorder.
• Common side effects: nausea, dry mouth, dizziness, insomnia, sweating, fatigue, constipation.
• Cautions: may raise blood pressure; taper slowly to avoid withdrawal symptoms.
• Pregnancy: decisions should be made with a clinician after weighing risks/benefits; do not start/stop without advice.
• This file is general education only.
