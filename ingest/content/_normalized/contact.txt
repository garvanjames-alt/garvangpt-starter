GET IN TOUCH 
As one of the last remaining truly independent pharmacy’s in Cork, our focus is on patient care and providing a personal service. If you have questions about your condition or your medicines, just ask, we are more than happy to help.We are located in the residential area of Broadale, Maryborough Hill,&nbsp; Douglas. Lynch's Pharmacy is bright, well-stocked and has a kids corner where younger children can safely play.There is ample parking outside the door. We dispense private prescriptions and all the government drug schemes - Medical Card (GMS), Drug Payment Scheme (DPS), Long Term Illness (LTI), Hi-tech and Hardship Scheme - and we always welcome new patients.
